About Us

Be Stylish Salon was founded 4 years ago with the aim of providing world-class salon experience to clients. The brand is managed by Mrs. Sangita Choudhary, Professional Makeup artist and Managing Director, Be Stylish Salon.

Over the years, Be Stylish Salon has been a pioneer in providing customers with luxurious experience for Hair, Skin, Hands & Feet, and Makeup with best-in-class products, finest amenities and professionally trained staff.

Founder

Mrs. Sangita Choudhary is the Creative & Managing Director of Be Stylish Salon, Professional Makeup Artist & Hair Stylist. From working as a helper staff in salons to establishing her own brand , she is an inspiration for many artists who desire to be trained under her guidance. She learned the art from international prominent artists.

“I just love faces,” she says. “Every single person's face is different, so as a makeup artist, your canvas is always different.”
